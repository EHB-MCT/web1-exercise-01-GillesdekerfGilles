Roast Chicken with Chile-Basil Vinaigrette, Charred Broccoli, and Potatoes

  • Roast Chicken with Chile-Basil Vinaigrette, Charred Broccoli, and Potatoes
  • selfmade Pizza
  • Oven baked patatoe

Ingredienten

  • 1 (4-pound) whole chicken, backbone removed, split through breastbone
  • Barbeque sauce
  • Tomaat
  • 6 medium garlic heads
  • ⅓ cup Dijon mustard

Bereiding

  • Bring 4 quarts water to a simmer in a large stockpot over medium-high.
  • Preheat oven to 375°F. Trim 1/2 inch from top of each head of garlic to expose cloves.
  • Squeeze cloves from 5 garlic heads into a food processor; discard skins.
